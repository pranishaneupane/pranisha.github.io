Let's take a look at the foods

Daal-bhaat

Daal-bhaat is the national dish of Nepal. Daal is lentil soup and Bhaat is rice, together they make the best comfort food known to every Nepali out there.

Mo:Mo

Mo:Mo is a dumpling with Nepali papa and Tibetan mama. From street foods to high-end restaurants, it is a staple and a way of life.

Ramen

Nothing can go wrong with a nice bowl of ramen. If you're a pro - make it fancy, if you're not - have it your way. It's yummy both ways.

Fried Chicken

What's tastier than a double fried, thick battered, crip exterior and juicy fried chicken? Nothing comes to mind. Let's not look at calories for a second here ;)

Naan

Naan is that one extremely social friend who gets along with everyone yet only hangs out with butter chicken. And it's good even without a pair.

Brownie

If I could bake, I'd make 100 brownies and manage to eat them within a single day. My 9-year-old cousin nicknamed me 'Prownie' because it rhymes with brownie. They're both sweet <3
